Vestige

Henry, a beetle that migrated from the desert all the way to the city of Bogota, Colombia. He embarks on a voyage, working and surviving through recycling having to cope with the weight of his and his wife's struggles. In an unexpected matter he was impacted with a bomb causing him to lose one of his extremities. As a victim of this bomb outrage he looks to share his story to compensate the damages that this unfair war has brought upon him. Encouraged by his resilience, he fights for a worthy life with the hope of being heard, leaving the memory to prevent reoccurrence nor oblivion.
