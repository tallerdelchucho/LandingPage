Development

Nina & Bonifacio

by René Castillo

Nina and Bonifacio, "Private Eyes", is a fun-filled Stop Motion animated series for kids aged 4 to 6. Join Nina and Bonifacio, a wonderful duo that love to help others, and meet their soft crafty world. Nina always has and answer and Bonifacio always falls into troubles. Follow them into their adventures and learn how they always find a way to solve problems and puzzles, meanwhile discover new things about the world, nature and themselves.

Audience: 4 to 6 yrs. old
Technique: Stop motion Fabrics
Genre: Adventure fantasy
Country: Mexico
