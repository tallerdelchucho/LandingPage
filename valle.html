The Valley of Faceless Men

In a forgotten corner of the cosmos lies a village under the yoke of a ruthless monarch who has stolen the identity of its inhabitants by stripping them of their faces. When a woman discovers that a baby is capable of restoring the stolen faces, an unrelenting pursuit ensues.
