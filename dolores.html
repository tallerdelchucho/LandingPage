Dolores

by Cecilia Andalón

Dolores is a 7 years-old girl that only wants to play, she flees from a fire demon and by mistake falls into a “Tumba de Tiro”, a mortuary compound where she meets two spooky skull ladies made from corn leaves, that wish Dolores to stay, but a little armadillo will try everything to get her out of there.
