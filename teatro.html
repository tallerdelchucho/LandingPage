Secret Teather

An old woman offers a lock of her hair to one of her artifacts; a magical puppet theater. She turns the crank, and it gives her a gift. The woman keeps turning it, and the puppet theater absorbs her into a dream, starting a performance of puppets and insects where her presence will illuminate life.
