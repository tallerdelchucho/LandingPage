Tale From the Sea

A young boy searches for his missing father at sea and arrives at a mysterious island, an enchanted place where things have come to life. In this fantastical realm, he faces the greatest challenge of his life: rescuing his mother from the island's tyrant, a ruthless computer from the 80s.
