Cuento de Mar

Stranded on a magical island where discarded things have come to life, a highly imaginative child joins a rebellious group of obsolete objects to rescue his mother from the island's tyrant—an ruthless 1980s computer with eternal resentment towards humans. In his epic odyssey, our hero uncovers secrets of the sea and grasps its true purpose.
