Short film

3 minutes

Pre production

Born again

In an unfinished room Danitza, a young mother, places a small box on top of a piece of furniture. From the box she takes out two bunny-shaped onesies that magically come to life. The two bunny onesies run into the room and finally enter a closet. Danitza tries to reach them and enters a space parallel to the room where the room is finished and has toys all around. A sound breaks the magical atmosphere, transforming the space into a dark and sad place. Danitza, having lost her children, plunges into a deep depression from which she must get out of and continue with her life.
